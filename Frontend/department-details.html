Our Neurological Services

Our neurological services include advanced diagnostic imaging, EEG monitoring, lumbar punctures, nerve conduction studies, and a full range of treatment options tailored to each patient's needs.

Epilepsy Treatment

Our specialists use advanced diagnostic tools including EEG monitoring and video telemetry to accurately diagnose and effectively manage all types of epilepsy, from focal to generalized seizures.

Diagnostic Imaging

We offer state-of-the-art MRI, CT, and PET scanning capabilities for precise diagnosis of neurological conditions, with results interpreted by our expert radiologists.

Stroke Prevention

Our comprehensive stroke prevention program includes risk assessment, lifestyle counseling, medication management, and advanced imaging to identify and address potential risks before they become emergencies.

Movement Disorders

Our movement disorder specialists provide expert diagnosis and treatment for conditions such as Parkinson's disease, essential tremor, dystonia, and other movement-related disorders.

Sleep Disorders

We diagnose and treat a wide range of sleep disorders including insomnia, sleep apnea, restless leg syndrome, and narcolepsy using advanced sleep study technology.

Memory Care

Our memory care program provides comprehensive evaluation and treatment for Alzheimer's disease, dementia, and other memory-related conditions, with a focus on maintaining quality of life.

Leading Neurological Expertise

Our department is led by internationally recognized neurologists and neurosurgeons who bring decades of combined experience in diagnosing and treating the most complex neurological conditions.
