What services does Salama Clinic offer?

Salama Clinic provides a wide range of healthcare services including cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, orthopedics, emergency care, and laboratory testing. Our experienced medical team is dedicated to delivering personalized care for every patient.

02

Are there specialists available at the clinic?

Yes, our clinic has a team of board-certified specialists across multiple departments. Each specialist brings years of experience and is committed to providing the highest quality of care using the latest medical techniques and technology.

How can I contact the clinic for emergencies?

For emergencies, you can call our 24/7 emergency hotline directly. Our emergency department is always staffed and ready to provide immediate medical attention. You can also visit our clinic in person during operating hours.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our front desk, visiting our website, or using our online booking system. Our staff will help you find a convenient time with the right specialist for your needs.
